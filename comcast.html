I worked with Comcast xFinity xFi team as their UX Design Intern from June 2019 – August 2019.

During my time there, I worked on the xFi app, conducting user research to determine mental models and categorizations in order to redesign the information hierarchy on a core component of the app.

I had to design for many unknowns and technical edge cases, which became the hardest part about the experience. To overcome this, I used the insights I gained from user research to guide my iterations.


I have signed a NDA, but feel free to reach out to me here to learn more about my work with xFi. You can view more about the company on their website.
